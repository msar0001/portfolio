About Us

I'm a self taught photographer/director who has been specializing in food & drink for 10 years. I'm currently splitting my time working Ottawa

So how did I get here?

Originally a graphic designer, I dove head first into photography after the purchase of my first camera. I found the transition into photography to be a very natural progression for me. As a designer you’re constantly paying attention to all the small details as well as the bigger picture…photography is no different.

I try to keep one simple quote in mind when I’m shooting food: “People eat with their eyes first”

Shooting is my passion. I don't think I'll ever stop. If I'm not shooting, I'm planning a shoot or in the studio editing yesterdays shots. Want to chat about your project? Drop me a line here.
